3 Steps to Glow

1
Lather up! 🫧✨

Wet your face and apply the cushioning cleanser. This will let the wand glide effortlessly over your skin.

2
Glide to glow 💎✨

Glide the wand in a circular motion to remove facial hair, dead skin and impurities.
‍

3
Rinse and radiate 💦🌟

Rinse your face. Get on with your day.
